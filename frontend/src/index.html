About Alana Terry

I'm a Christian fiction author who believes in the power of storytelling to inspire, challenge, and encourage faith. Through my Kennedy Stern Christian Suspense series, I explore themes of justice, forgiveness, and God's unfailing love in the midst of life's darkest moments.

As the host of the Praying Christian Women podcast, I have the privilege of connecting with women around the world, sharing stories of faith, and encouraging deeper prayer lives. Whether through my novels or podcast, my mission is to point hearts toward Jesus.
